The entrance has two security gates and is adjacent to the circulation desk. The Gimbel Design Library's1 rules2 are posted on the window. The catalogue and additional resources can be accessed through the research stations. Books can be returned or checked out at the circulation desk or taken upstairs to the quiet study area. Additional research help can be requested at the reference desk.
1
The Gimbel Design Library is open from 8-9pm Monday through Thursday, 8-7pm on Friday and 12-6pm on the weekends.
2
No cutting tools. No drinks. No food. No art supplies. No phone calls.
